Learning is a complex activity that involves many cognitive processes

These cognitive processes are negatively impacted by fatigue and eye strain. 

Impaired Cognitive Function

Fatigue slows down the brain's ability to process and store information.

Reduced Attention Span

When you're tired, maintaining focus becomes challenging. 

Lowered Motivation

Fatigue often comes with a lack of energy, which can reduce your motivation to engage with learning materials actively.

Visual Discomfort

Eye strain can cause symptoms like dry eyes, blurred vision, and headaches, making it uncomfortable to read or look at screens for extended periods.

Decreased Reading Efficiency

Difficulty focusing due to eye strain can slow down reading speed and comprehension, leading to frustration and decreased learning effectiveness.

Impaired Memory and Concentration

Stress releases cortisol, a hormone that can interfere with the brain's ability to form new memories and concentrate on tasks.

Reduced Cognitive Flexibility

High stress levels can make it difficult to think creatively or adapt to new learning situations.

Problem

Children often fail to recognize fatigue or digital eye strain in time, which decreases their learning efficiency.

Solution

EyesApp’s eye-tracking technology monitors fatigue and digital eye strain, alerting children when these issues arise.

We help children avoid these challenges and optimize their study process for enhanced learning.

EyesApp teaches children how to learn efficiently.
