EyesApp tailors the learning process for every child based on their
individual features by using eye tracking technology.

Cross-platform software

EyesApp monitors your child’s learning activities across all devices in the background mode.

To enhance learning, our eye-tracking technology monitors key processes such as:

  • Lack of focus
  • Fatigue
  • Low engagement
  • Cognitive overload
  • Eye strain

When the app detects

  • Lack of focus
  • Eye strain
  • Fatigue

It alerts your child

  • To take a break
  • Review the material
  • Get an alternative explanation

Our recommendations help children to enhance their learning and prevent:

  • Cognitive overload
  • Digital eye strain
  • Learning-related stress

EyesApp provides essential information on learning process to you and your child in real time.
